Lily's Brief Bio

Lily was born on September 11, 2012, at the Boulder Community Foothills Hospital

The BCFH is the newest annex of the Boulder Hospital network.

Lily wakes up 07:30 every morning.


Lily was born with blue eyes and with blond hair.


A list of Lily's favorite foods:
  • Avocado
  • Oatmeal
  • Cheese
  • Apple Sauce
Lily loves her bottle and here are the steps to make it to her liking:
  1. Microwave water 6 oz. of water for 40 seconds
  2. Add three scoops of formula into her bottle
  3. Pour the heated water into bottle to the 6 oz. mark
  4. Stir and serve!
